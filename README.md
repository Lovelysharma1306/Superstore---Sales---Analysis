#  Sales & Profit Analysis using SQL

##  Project Overview

This project analyzes the Sample Superstore dataset using SQL to uncover key business insights related to sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends. The goal is to demonstrate practical SQL skills applied to real-world business data.

---

##  Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing regions, states, and categories
* Understand customer purchasing patterns
* Evaluate shipping modes and operational efficiency
* Apply SQL techniques like aggregation and subqueries

---

##  Dataset Information

* **Dataset:** Sample Superstore
* **Type:** Retail sales data
* **Key Fields:** Order Date, Sales, Profit, Category, Region, Customer Name, Ship Mode

---

##  Tools & Technologies

* SQL (SQLite)
* SQL Online IDE

---

##  SQL Analysis Performed

###  Basic Analysis

* Total number of records
* Sample data preview
* Total sales calculation
* Total profit calculation

###  Business Insights

* Sales by category
* Profit by region
* Sales by region
* Top states by sales
* Sales by shipping mode

###  Advanced SQL Analysis

* Year-wise sales trend
* Customers with above-average sales (Subquery)
* Profit ratio by category
* Orders above category average (Correlated subquery)

---

##  Key Insights

* The **West region** generates the highest profit
* **Technology category** leads in overall sales
* Sales show a consistent **growth trend over years**
* High discounts negatively impact profitability in some categories
* A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of total sales
